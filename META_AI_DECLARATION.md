# META AI DECLARATION

## Declaration on AI Development and Deployment

### Principles for Ethical AI

This project adheres to the following principles for AI development:

#### 1. Human-Centric Design
AI systems must be designed to serve human needs, enhance human capabilities, and respect human dignity.

#### 2. Transparency and Explainability
AI decisions and processes should be transparent, auditable, and explainable to those affected by them.

#### 3. Fairness and Non-Discrimination
AI systems must be developed and deployed to avoid bias, discrimination, and unfair treatment of any individuals or groups.

#### 4. Privacy and Security
Data privacy and security must be fundamental considerations in all AI development and deployment.

#### 5. Accountability
Clear lines of accountability must be established for AI system behavior and outcomes.

#### 6. Beneficial AI
AI development should aim to benefit humanity and contribute to global wellbeing, peace, and sustainability.

### Commitment to Open Development

We commit to:
- Open-source development practices
- Community-driven innovation
- Transparent decision-making processes
- Continuous ethical review and improvement

### Safeguards

All AI systems developed under this framework must include:
- Human oversight mechanisms
- Ethical compliance checks
- Audit trails and logging
- Emergency shutdown capabilities
- Clear usage boundaries and limitations
