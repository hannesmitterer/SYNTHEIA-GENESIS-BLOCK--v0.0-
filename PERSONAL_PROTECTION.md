# PERSONAL PROTECTION POLICY

## Protection of Individual Rights and Privacy

### Purpose

This policy establishes safeguards for individuals interacting with or affected by AI systems developed under the SYNTHEIA GENESIS BLOCK framework.

### Individual Rights

Every individual has the right to:

1. **Privacy**: Personal data will be protected and used only with explicit consent
2. **Autonomy**: Freedom to choose whether and how to interact with AI systems
3. **Transparency**: Understand when interacting with AI and how it makes decisions
4. **Access**: Request information about data collected and decisions made
5. **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
6. **Deletion**: Request deletion of personal data (right to be forgotten)
7. **Human Review**: Request human review of AI-made decisions affecting them
8. **Non-Discrimination**: Protection from discriminatory AI practices

### Data Protection Principles

1. **Data Minimization**: Collect only necessary data
2. **Purpose Limitation**: Use data only for stated purposes
3. **Storage Limitation**: Retain data only as long as necessary
4. **Security**: Implement robust security measures
5. **Confidentiality**: Maintain confidentiality of personal information

### Consent Requirements

- Clear and specific consent required for data collection
- Opt-in rather than opt-out for sensitive data
- Easy withdrawal of consent at any time
- No penalty for refusing consent

### Vulnerable Populations

Special protections for:
- Children and minors
- Elderly individuals
- People with disabilities
- Marginalized communities

### Redress Mechanisms

Individuals may:
- File complaints about privacy violations
- Request investigation of concerns
- Seek remedy for harms caused
- Access independent appeals processes

### Enforcement

Violations of this policy will result in:
- Immediate investigation
- Corrective action
- Public disclosure of violations
- Potential project suspension
